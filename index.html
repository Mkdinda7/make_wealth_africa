Agribusiness

25-40% Annual Returns

From organic farming to food processing and export, agriculture remains the most profitable sector with government incentives.

Best Entry: $1,000 - $10,000
Govt Support: Tax holidays, equipment subsidies
Hotspots: Ghana, Nigeria, Côte d'Ivoire

Petroleum & Gas

18-30% Annual Returns

With significant reserves, West Africa's oil and gas sector offers lucrative opportunities in exploration, refining and distribution.

Best Entry: $20,000 - $100,000
Govt Support: Cost sharing, priority forex access
Hotspots: Nigeria, Ghana, Senegal

Mining

30-60% Annual Returns

Gold, bauxite, iron ore, and diamonds mining present substantial investment opportunities with high returns.

Best Entry: $5,000 - $50,000
Govt Support: Free geological data, reduced royalties
Hotspots: Ghana, Mali, Senegal, Burkina Faso

Technology

40-100%+ Annual Returns

Fintech, agritech, e-commerce, and SaaS startups are attracting massive venture capital and generating exponential returns.

Best Entry: $5,000 - $50,000
Govt Support: Tech hubs, tax incentives, grants
Hotspots: Nigeria, Ghana, Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire
